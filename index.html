Distribution & Operations

Yash Raj Films

India's Premier Film Studio

About

Yash Raj Films doesn't release movies. It releases events. Working inside the distribution pipeline for Saiyaara, War 2, and Mandala Murders meant understanding how India's most powerful production house moves a film from production slate to sold-out screens — and what happens in every negotiation in between. Exhibitor relationships, agreement structuring, revenue strategy across theatrical and OTT windows. The same client management discipline that runs billion-dollar ad deals — applied to the biggest releases in Indian cinema.

What I Did

  • Managed theatrical and OTT distribution operations across three major releases.
  • Handled exhibitor relationships spanning multiplex chains and single-screen circuits — negotiations, agreement structuring, and revenue share conversations.
  • Coordinated day-to-day and backend operations across the full release window.
  • Researched box office data, demographics, and territory-level ticket pricing to inform revenue maximization strategy.
